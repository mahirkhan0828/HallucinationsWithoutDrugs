Hallucinations Without Drugs

While experiencing visual illusions do not show personality traits as some may suggest, it can be a fun encounter when trying to mess with your senses. After viewing a moving stimulus for a while, and then focusing your eyes on a stationary object, you can encounter the motion aftereffect. Neurons get used to the movements and adapt to the impulses.

Instructions

Below are some illusions that will tamper with your perception. They are separated into four levels with increasing intensities. When watching these videos, make sure that you are staring at the middle of the illusion, which usually consists of a dot and for the full effect, do not blink. After viewing these stimuli, find a wall, or a stationary object.

WARNING

While optical illusions are proven not to harm your eyes, if you stare at an image on the computer screen for too long it can cause:

  • Eye Strain
  • Sore/Itchy Eyes
  • Blurred Vision
  • Dry or Watery Eyes
